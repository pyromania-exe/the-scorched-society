Faction Overview

The Scorched Society is a coalition of survivors who embraced the harsh, fire-scarred regions to carve out a domain of industry, salvage, and ritual flame. They prize endurance, resource control, and a strict code measured in ash. This wiki collects the faction's history, notable members, doctrines, gameplay mechanics, and roleplaying hooks.

Philosophy — Order Born from Ash
The Scorched Society is not a government. It is not a religion. It is a creed—an oath sworn by those who believe that peace is not a dream, but a discipline. They believe that law is the highest good—not oppressive rule, but structured equity, consistency, and protection for all, even in the worst of times.
They uphold five founding principles:
1.Justice Above Power – No one is above the law, not even the Society itself.
2.Equity in All Things – Resolve disputes not with might, but with fairness.
3.Memory of the Flame – Never forget the consequences of unrestrained ambition.
4.Service to the Voiceless – Protect those who cannot protect themselves.
5.Structure Breeds Survival – Chaos is death. Order is life. Legacy — The Law That Burns Bright
