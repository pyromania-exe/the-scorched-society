The Scorched Society Laws

Official regulations and guidelines of our faction

Home Laws

A Class A Laws

  • Unnecessary conflict, verbal hostility, or behavior that breaks public unity.
  • Minor insults or refusal to show basic respect to leaders or fellow members.
  • Ignoring votes, consensus, or rulings that apply to all members.
  • Failure to perform assigned roles or duties when physically and mentally able.
  • Using one’s influence, strength, or station to intimidate without just cause.
  • Going back on promises or agreements that do not endanger others.

B Class B Laws

  • Deliberately refusing to follow a lawful command or enforce agreed law.
  • Spreading lies or conspiracies that weaken faith in the faction’s structure.
  • Attempting to gain rank, role, or control outside of sanctioned paths or proper attending of training sessions.
  • Attacking another member unintentionally (Say sorry, if you don’t this will apply.
  • Abandoning sworn duties or roles that protect others or uphold the law.

C Class C Laws

  • Acting to betray, overthrow, or destroy the Faction or its people.
  • Killing a member with murderous intent.
  • Deliberately causing destruction, mass fear, or lawless violence.
  • Using one's station or strength to dominate, exploit, or enslave.
