Leaders

  • pyromania - The leader and founder of the scorched society as well as commander of the disciples of repremandation.
  • REDACTED - Leads the counsil of law and order as the head judge and assists as the co-leader of the faction.
  • WEST - commander of the scorched attack unit and elite warriors of the infernal knights.

Support Ranks

  • Supervises squads and enforces discipline and standards.
  • Provides technical or specialist skills such as medics or engineers.
  • Junior non-commissioned officer; leads small teams and mentors recruits.

Civilian Ranks

  • Community-elected leaders responsible for local governance and liaison with command.
  • Represent factions of citizens in councils and advise leadership on civilian matters.
  • General members of the community with rights and responsibilities under faction law.
